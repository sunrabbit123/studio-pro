# Language settings

### Step 1

Hover over the left navigation bar and access the settings page.

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/language-settings/step-1-1.png)

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/language-settings/step-1-2.png)

<br />
<br />

### Step 2

Click on the second item ‘Change Language / 언어 변경’ and select your preferred language from the dropdown menu. (Currently, Korean and English are supported.)

![](/studio-pro/images/docs/getting-started/language-settings/step-2-1.png)
